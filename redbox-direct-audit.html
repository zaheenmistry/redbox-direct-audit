04 — Growth Opportunities

The highest-impact moves available

01 — Google Ads
Own Your Category in Paid Search
Neither brand currently bids on the keywords your ideal customers are actively searching. With low competition and highly specific long-tail terms, cost-per-click in this niche is low — €0.80–€2.50 typically — making paid search extremely cost-effective for lead generation.
↑ Est. 15–30 qualified leads/month from Day 1
02 — Content SEO
GLS as the Go-To Research Resource
Researchers who buy scientific instruments typically spend weeks researching before purchasing. A systematic content programme on GLS — product guides, application notes, comparison articles — captures that research phase and builds the trust that converts to enquiries.
↑ Est. +40% organic traffic in 6 months
03 — Video & Podcast
Turn Conference Content into a Content Engine
The client interview videos from SfN are exactly what a B2B life science podcast needs. A monthly "Life Science Global" podcast — short, expert-led, easily produced — distributed on Spotify, Apple Podcasts and LinkedIn would build a loyal audience of exactly the people RBD wants to reach.
↑ Brand authority + inbound lead channel
04 — Email Database
Activate the Scientific Database
A segmented database of life science researchers is one of the most valuable B2B assets imaginable. Monthly segmented campaigns — new product announcements, application spotlights, event invitations — to a warm, relevant audience will consistently outperform every other channel on ROI.
↑ Highest ROI channel — immediate activation
05 — Looker Studio
Real-Time Visibility Across All Channels
Build a single Looker Studio dashboard pulling from GA4, Google Ads, Search Console, and LinkedIn. Management gets a live view of what's working without waiting for monthly reports — faster decisions, better budget allocation, and clear attribution from spend to lead.
↑ Data-driven decisions from Week 1
06 — LinkedIn Ads
Target Manufacturers Ready to Expand
LinkedIn's targeting allows reaching "VP Sales at a US life science manufacturer with 50–500 employees" directly. A small, well-targeted campaign — "Expanding into Europe? Here's how 12 brands did it" — using RBD's success stories as lead magnets would generate highly qualified inbound enquiries.
↑ Direct pipeline growth for RBD core service
